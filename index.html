A little about myself...

Developer, Rower,
Outdoorsman, Skier

I recently graduated Tufts University with a BS in Computer Science. Presently I live in San Francisco and thorougly enjoy all that the Bay Area has to offer. On weekend you'll likely find me skiing in Tahoe, or training for an upcoming half marathon. I presently work as a Software Engineer on the Kiwi Web Team.

Originally from Boise Idaho, I came to Massachusetts to study at Tufts in 2011, and have spent the past few years exploring New England and the East Coast. When back in Idaho I enjoy many outdoor activities including backpacking, fly-fishing and whitewater rafting. I have traveled to 42 states and the District of Columbia as well as 16 foreign countries.
